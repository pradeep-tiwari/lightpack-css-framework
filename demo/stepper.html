CSS Classes

  • .stepper - Container for steps
  • .step - Individual step
  • .step-active - Current active step
  • .step-completed - Completed step (use ✓ in circle)
  • .step-circle - Circle with number/icon
  • .step-label - Step label text
  • .step-line - Connecting line between steps
